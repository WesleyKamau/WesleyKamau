I'd like to tell you about my favorite dessert everrrrrrrr, Butter Cake. Specifically the one served at California Pizza Kitchen. It's a warm, buttery vanilla cake topped with Haagen Dazs ice cream. One of my favorite things about it, though, is the fact that it's meant to be shared with others. I've been wanting to make it myself for a while now (the damn thing is $13 at the restaurant), and I've finally come around to making my own recipe. This cake is amazing, and I encourage you to try the real deal at some point in your life. But for now, I've made one for you. I hope you enjoy it, and I hope you share it with someone special. Also, the ice cream is not a suggestion. Make sure you get some ice cream. Trust me.



How to warm the cake up


Notes


  • Keep the cake frozen until you're ready to eat it. This is how they do it at the restaurant, so... yeah.
  • Get some ice cream. They use Vanilla Haagen Dazs at the restaurant, but any vanilla ice cream will do.

Instructions:


  1. Remove from packaging and put the frozen butter cake on a microwave-safe plate.
  2. Microwave on high for 45 seconds to 1 minute. The cake should be HOT!
  3. Top the the hot Butter Cake with a scoop of Ice Cream.
  4. Grab some spoons and enjoy with friends
